About me

Studies

I am a third year Honors student at Rochester Institute of technology, currently majoring in Game Design and Development. I am also pursing minors in Math and Computer Science.

Passion

When I think about what kind of work excites me, the first thing that always comes to mind is coding and digital design. I enjoy the challenges that come with learning new languages, the creation of algorithms to solve problems, and the vast utility that coding can provide. I enjoy creating new enviroments in game engines, modeling and animating unique characters, and creating my own spritesheets in 2D games.

Hobbies

In my free time I like to experiment with special effects, often in the Unity engine. I'm especially quite fond of shaders. Outside of my academics, I volunteer frequently through the Honors program at my university. Creating posters for events and even running a few of them.
